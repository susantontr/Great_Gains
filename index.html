What This App Does

Gauge is a personal workout tracking app. It lets you log exercises, track progress over time, and visualize your progress. We take your privacy seriously and only collect what is necessary to make the app work.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Your email address (used to sign in and contact you if needed)
  • Your display name / username (shown within the app)

Workout Data

When you use the app, we store:

  • Exercises you log (name, sets, reps, weight, date)
  • Custom exercise variations you create
  • Weekly training snapshots and progress history

Feedback and Bug Reports

If you voluntarily submit feedback or report a bug through the app, we collect the message you write along with your email address so we can follow up if needed.

What We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your location, contacts, camera or microphone data, health records, financial information, or any data from other apps on your device.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide and operate the Gauge service
  • Save and sync your workout history across sessions
  • Respond to feedback or bug reports you submit

We do not use your data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or share it with anyone outside of the services listed below.

How Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored using Firebase (a Google service). Firebase provides authentication and database services. Your data is stored on Google's servers in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not operate our own servers. All data is processed and stored by Firebase on our behalf.

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account through the app (Settings → Delete Account), all of your data — including your workout history, custom exercises, and progress snapshots — is permanently and immediately deleted from our database. This action cannot be undone.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time (via Settings → Delete Account)
  • Contact us with any privacy-related questions or requests

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [YOUR EMAIL].

Children's Privacy

Gauge is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will update the Effective Date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
